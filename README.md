About ClearCalc

ClearCalc is a simple, transparent, and practical global loan and tax calculator.

We are committed to making complex financial calculations easier to understand. ClearCalc provides convenient tools for estimating mortgages, auto loans, and personal income taxes. Whether you are planning to buy a home, purchase a car, or understand your potential tax obligations, ClearCalc helps you get clear estimates quickly.

ClearCalc currently supports the United States, Japan, Singapore, Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, and France. It is available in Simplified Chinese, English, Japanese, French, and German, making financial planning more accessible to users around the world.

Our goal is simple:

Make complex calculations clear and easy to understand.

No registration is required. With a clean interface, clear results, and reliable data sources, ClearCalc provides a convenient starting point for your loan and tax planning.

Disclaimer: ClearCalc’s results are estimates provided for planning and informational purposes only. They do not constitute financial, tax, legal, loan, or investment advice. Please refer to local government authorities and financial institutions for the latest policies and official calculations.
